HISTORY OF US

Where We Started

We began our journey in 1908 through the merging of three holiness movements. From those early days, we rapidly grew, reaching across the globe with our missions and church planting efforts.
As we expanded, we established numerous educational institutions and significantly grew our missionary work, becoming a major force in global evangelism and education.

Where We Are Now

Today, we’re proud to operate in over 160 countries, actively engaged in global missions, compassionate ministries, and educational initiatives.
We face challenges such as declining enrollments and financial pressures, but we are adapting our strategies to ensure we maintain our impact and relevance.

Where We Are Going

Looking ahead, we are embracing innovative digital outreach and mission strategies to connect with new generations and meet emerging global needs.
We’re focusing on enhancing our global collaborations and optimizing resources to further amplify our worldwide mission and effectiveness.
